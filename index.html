DISINTEGRATION

A series of interactive Gaussian splat objects.

Each piece is a spatial gesture created through camera movement within an artist-constructed 3D world. The resulting object is a translation of movement into volume and form.

Collectors can experience and play with each work via the live viewer or download. The object is fixed; perception is not.

Collector access Each collector receives a 1:1 session with the artist and a guided virtual visit through the constructed world from which the works were captured.
